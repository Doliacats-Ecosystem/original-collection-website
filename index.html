Dolia Cats is a limited collection of 9,999 gorgeous programmatically generated cat-themed NFTs on the Ethereum blockchain.

Made from a variety of different objects, heads, necks and face accessories, body colours, backgrounds, eyes, and expressions, each Dolia Cat NFT is completely unique.

Owning a Dolia Cat NFT will give you exclusive power in the Meowverse, the only metaverse for NFT cats, and the most tightly knit community out there!

Meowverse

The Meowverse is the metaverse with its own tokenomilks, built exclusively for cool cats and kittens. Here, you can let your imagination run wild, anything is possible.

You see, when you own a Dolia Cat NFT, you’re given a seat on the Meowverse board of directors, giving you a say and vote on what happens next in the play2earn MeoWorld. You’ll also get a free 3D cat avatar to explore the Meowverse – how cool is that!

In the Meowverse, you’ll be able to enjoy play2earn games alone, or with friends, buy, sell and trade Dolia Cat NFTs, collect cool upgrades, and buy up plots of land to store all your precious kitties. You get to influence your Meowverse experience through the in-game actions you take.
